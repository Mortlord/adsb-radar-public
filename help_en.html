Favorites &
Notifications

01 — What are Favorites?

With favorites you can keep track of specific flights or airlines. As soon as a favorite enters your alert zone, you receive a Telegram notification.

Callsign or Prefix?

You can save either a full callsign or just a prefix (the first 2–3 letters) as a favorite.

Examples LH All Lufthansa flights (LH400, LH123, …)
LH400 This specific flight only
EZY All easyJet flights
RYR All Ryanair flights
DLH Lufthansa (ICAO code)
Tip: Tap any aircraft in the app — the callsign is shown in the detail panel and can be saved as a favorite directly.
02 — Set up Telegram

Notifications are sent via Telegram — a free messenger app. You do not need an account with ADS-B Radar.

Step by Step

  • 01 Install Telegram on your smartphone or open web.telegram.org in your browser.
  • 02 Search for @adsb_radar_bot and open the chat.
  • 03 Send /start — the bot immediately replies with your personal activation code.
  • 04 Open the ⭐ FAVORITES panel in the app and enter your activation code in the field provided.
  • 05 Add at least one favorite and tap Save & Activate.
Treat your activation code like a password and do not share it. It securely links the app to your Telegram chat.
✈ Open @adsb_radar_bot
03 — When do notifications arrive?

Alert Zone

You are notified when a favorite enters your configured alert zone. You can set the radius at the top of the app (default: 20 nm).

Active hours 08:00 – 23:59 your local time
No notifications at night – wherever you are

Cooldown

At most one notification is sent per callsign every 5 minutes — even if the aircraft is detected multiple times in the alert zone.

App does not need to be open

The server automatically checks every 5 minutes whether a favorite is in your zone. The app does not need to be open — it is enough to have opened it once with your activation code entered.

⚠ If you change location (e.g. while travelling), open the app briefly and tap Save & Activate again so the server knows your new position.
04 — Daily Evening Report

Every evening at 23:59 the bot sends a daily summary of your favorites.

Favorites

All favorites that entered your alert zone today — with distance, direction and time. After the report, the favorites list is cleared for the next day.

Example report ⭐ Favorites today – 2 unique callsigns
  THY2AY – 10.0 nm SE (13:40)
  SWR123 – 8.5 nm N (17:22)

Bot Commands

Available commands in the Telegram chat /start — Show your activation code
/stats — Most frequent visitors in your alert zone today
/unbekannt — Show unknown callsign groups
05 — Delete your data

You can delete your stored data at any time. Open the ⭐ FAVORITES panel in the app and tap:

🗑 Delete my data (GDPR)

This removes your location, activation code, favorites and notification history from the server. Local browser data can be deleted via your browser settings.
